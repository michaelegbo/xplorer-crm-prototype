How The Product Works

Queue to decision
1. Login by roleStaff, admins, reviewers, AI governance, sector authors, field reps, and portal users start from separate role entries.
2. Work from scoped queuesEach role lands in its own dashboard and sees permitted screens only.
3. Capture evidenceOnboarding, cases, inspections, exports, sector promotion, and sensitive reveals carry evidence state.
4. Govern outcomesMaker-checker, consent, degraded mode, AI boundaries, and tenant conformance protect regulated work.
